Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including your account details (email, password) and expense data you choose to log (amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions). We do not collect or store any sensitive financial information such as credit card numbers or bank account details.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. We use your expense information to generate summaries, charts, and insights about your spending patterns. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your expense data is stored securely on our servers. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not integrate with any third-party financial services or share your data with external parties. Your expense data remains within the Spendly application and is only accessible to you through your account.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal data at any time. You can export or delete all your expense data through your account settings. If you choose to delete your account, all associated data will be permanently removed.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us. We are committed to addressing any concerns you may have about your privacy.
